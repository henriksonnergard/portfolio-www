Faux Brand

Project description

Faux Brand started a few years back as an outlet for various shirt designs I've been scetching on. Most of them are heavily influenced by song lyrics and different graphic styles I've been trying out. Blending my interest in photography, typography and graphic design, Faux Brand is a side project where I experiment with different design ideas that later make their way into other projects.
